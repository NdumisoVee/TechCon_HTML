History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of developers and innovators passionate about the future of technology. Over the years, it has grown into a global event that connects thousands of tech enthusiasts each year.

Mission

Our mission is to inspire innovation, foster collaboration, and shape the future of technology through inclusive and forward-thinking discussions and demonstrations.

Past Speakers

  • Dr. Ada Simons

    AI researcher and ethical tech advocate. Known for her work on responsible machine learning.

  • Leo Tanaka

    Founder of GreenCode Labs, a pioneer in sustainable computing practices.

  • Monique Jones

    Cybersecurity strategist and author, leading efforts in digital privacy and security awareness.
